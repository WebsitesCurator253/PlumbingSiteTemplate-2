Available 24/7 for emergencies

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
For plumbing emergencies — burst pipes, flooding, sewage backup — we aim to be on-site within 60 minutes anywhere in the 253. We staff technicians around the clock so you're never waiting on hold or leaving a voicemail. Call us directly at (253) 555-0100.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all non-emergency plumbing work. A licensed technician will assess your situation, explain the options, and give you a flat-rate price before any work begins. No pressure, no surprises.
Are you licensed and insured in Washington State?
Absolutely. ProFlow Plumbing is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the State of Washington. All of our technicians hold current WA State plumber certifications. We're happy to provide proof of licensing and insurance upon request.
My water pressure is low — what causes that?
Low water pressure can be caused by several things: mineral buildup in pipes or fixtures, a partially closed shutoff valve, a failing pressure regulator, or even a hidden leak. It can also be a municipal supply issue. We'll diagnose the root cause quickly and give you a clear recommendation.
How do I know if I need a water heater repair or full replacement?
Generally, if your water heater is under 8–10 years old and has a single issue (like a faulty thermostat or heating element), a repair is usually cost-effective. If it's older, leaking from the tank itself, or breaking down repeatedly, replacement is often the smarter investment. We'll always tell you honestly which makes more sense for your situation.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all of the 253 and Pierce County — including Tacoma, Gig Harbor, Puyallup, Lakewood, University Place, Bonney Lake, Sumner, Auburn, Federal Way, and surrounding communities. Not sure if we cover your area? Just call and we'll let you know right away.
Do you work on weekends and holidays?
Yes — our emergency line is staffed 24/7, including weekends and all holidays. Standard service appointments are available Monday through Saturday. Emergency calls on Sundays and holidays may include an after-hours fee, which we'll always disclose upfront before dispatching a technician.
